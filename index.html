Padel Pro – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-09

Padel Pro (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Analytics data (via Firebase): app installs, sessions, device information (model, OS version), and in‑app events (e.g., matches played, purchases).
  • Advertising data (via Unity Ads/AdMob): ad impressions, clicks, approximate location for ad serving, device identifiers for ads (e.g., AAID), and diagnostics.
  • Purchases (via Google Play Billing): non‑sensitive purchase confirmations (SKU, amount, currency, status). Payments are processed by Google; we do not receive full payment details.

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve gameplay, stability, and performance.
  • Serve ads (including rewarded ads) and measure their effectiveness.
  • Process in‑app purchases and deliver purchased content.

Legal Basis & Consent

Where required, the app will request consent for analytics and ads (including personalized ads). You can change ad personalization settings in your device settings or within the app (where provided).

Sharing

We use third‑party services under their respective policies:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Unity Ads and/or Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing

We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention & Security

Analytics and ads data are retained according to each provider’s default policies. We use industry‑standard safeguards; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences aged 12+. It is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to request deletion.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
